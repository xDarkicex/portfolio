About me

I currently attend Fresno City College—working to get my Degree in Computer Science—and I aid a class at Fresno State for CSCI-130, teaching Go-lang. My dream career is to be a professional web designer on the front end and back end, and to write applications to provide easy learning for future generations. My specialties are primarily in web development. Something about creation drives me crazy and I love making my work come to life. This creation aspect has led me to a multitude of programming languages and paradigms. The more recents are Objective-C, Swift, and Golang. I particularly enjoy Object-Oriented Programming languages, although Go is not. Go, however, is definitely a fundamental part of modern day web programming.

I have also begun dabbling in photography and writing music. The picture of the ocean wave at the top of the page is actually one I took in Carmel, California. After now doing photography for several years, it has become a huge part of my life for many reasons. Some photographers who have influenced me include: Rolf Hicker from BC, Canada, my good friend, Casey Chumley, and my non-biological twin, Anya Wiggins.

The music side of me can really tell you the story of thousand souls, simply with instrumentals. Most of my musical influence comes from artists like Dream Theater, Periphery, Animals As Leaders, Coheed & Cambria, Anathema, and many others. I particularly enjoy playing extended range guitars, percussion instruments, and piano, however, those are not the only instruments I can play. My genres of choice would have to be Progressive Metal, Djent, Orchestral and Classical, Electronica, and Christian Rap.
